## How to actually use these

**This week:**
1. Pick ONE channel to start. Recommend Channel 1 (CCA) because it has the highest referral leverage.
2. Find 5 specific people in that channel (5 CCAs at ag retail in your region, or 5 wedding venues within 60 miles, etc).
3. Send the cold message Monday. Send the follow-up the following Monday. That's it.
4. Track responses in your tracking sheet (see next execution artifact).

**Reply protocol:**
- They reply yes: send the fact sheet / rate sheet / next step within 24 hours, no exception.
- They reply maybe / not now: thank them, ask when to check back, mark a calendar reminder.
- They reply no: thank them, ask if they know someone who'd find it useful. Move on.
- No reply after follow-up: drop them. Don't chase. Move to next 5.

**Build the pipeline:**
- Channel 1 first month, get 4-5 CCA reply-yes responses.
- Channel 2 second month, get 3 wedding venues booked for summer.
- Channel 3 third month, send to your 3 nearest mosquito districts.
- Channel 4 ongoing, every other month feature a vet operator.
- Channel 5 once: send to Adrian and Ag Drone Canada team, do the trade, move on.

That's the system. Five channels, real templates, ready to send.
