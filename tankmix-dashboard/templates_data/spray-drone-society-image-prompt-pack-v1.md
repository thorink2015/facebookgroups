# Spray Drone Society - Image Prompt Pack v1

All assets needed to launch. Each prompt is paste-ready for Claude Design (or DALL-E / Midjourney with minor tweaks).

---

## How to use

1. Open a new Claude chat with image generation enabled.
2. Find the asset you need below. Each one is a complete prompt with the master style block baked in.
3. Select the whole code block, copy, paste as your message. Claude generates the image.
4. If the first generation is off, reply in the same chat with the adjustment (for example, "move the drone closer to center" or "make the light warmer").
5. Download the PNG and save to your assets folder.

**Generation order (recommended):** Logo (from Style Guide) → Banner → Discovery Thumb → Channel Headers → Course Thumbs → Welcome Header → FB Banner → Founder Portrait.

Generate in this order because the logo and banner set the visual baseline, and every other asset should match the look you approved on those two.

## 6. Founder Portrait

Two paths. Use Path A (real photo of you) for the final About page and any place your face appears. Use Path B (AI placeholder) only until you take the real photo.

### Path A: Real photo direction (preferred)

**What you do, in plain steps:**

1. **Where:** outdoors, late afternoon, near a field or a parked truck. If Florida fields are not handy, a citrus grove, a hay field or even the edge of any green pasture works. Avoid your living room, an office, a coffee shop or a white wall.
2. **When:** the "golden hour" - the last 60 to 90 minutes before sunset. Side light, not noon overhead light.
3. **What to wear:** worn jeans, work boots, a button-up work shirt or a plain Henley, a faded ball cap or trucker hat. No suit, no polo with a logo, no plain t-shirt.
4. **Pose:** stand naturally, face mostly toward the camera but turned about 15 degrees off-axis (not dead-on, not full profile). One hand can rest in a pocket or on a hip. If you have a spray drone or a controller handy, hold it casually. Slight smile, eye contact with the lens.
5. **Camera:** modern phone is fine. Portrait Mode on. Friend stands about 6 to 8 feet away. Phone held at chest height, NOT above your head. Take 30 to 50 shots, vary the angle slightly.
6. **CENTERED rule:** put your face in the center third of the frame, eyes at about the upper third line. Your head should be in the middle of the image so it survives any crop.
7. **Backdrop:** field, sky, or a parked truck in the soft background. No clutter behind you.

After you have the photo, you can drop it into Claude or Photoshop to lightly color-grade it to match the brand palette (warmer side, slightly more saturated greens).

## Iteration tips

If a Claude Design generation doesn't hit, reply in the same chat with a SHORT specific fix. Examples that work:
- "Make the drone bigger and closer to the camera."
- "Lower the angle, shoot from waist height."
- "Less saturated, more dusty and faded."
- "The man looks too clean. Add dust and sweat to his shirt."
- "Replace the cornfield with a soybean field, shorter crop."
- "Move the operator closer to the drone, tighter framing."

Examples that DON'T work:
- "Make it cooler." (too vague)
- "Make it more brand." (Claude doesn't know your brand)
- "Just regenerate." (you'll get random results)
