## What we treat
Mosquitoes, ticks, gnats, and biting flies across your whole property. Spot weed cleanup along tree lines, pond edges, fence rows, and pasture. We do not treat lawn turf or flower beds unless asked.

## Why drone over a truck or fogger
- We treat your whole property from the air in about 45 minutes
- No truck on your lawn, no ruts, no spray crew during photos
- We hit the tree lines, shrub undersides, pond edges, and standing water where mosquitoes actually shelter, not just open grass
- One treatment lasts up to 17 days
- We can be in and out the morning before your big day

## How we time it
- Best window: 2 to 3 days before your event
- We can re-treat the morning of, weather permitting
- For multi-day events, we schedule one pre-event treatment and one mid-event touch-up

## What's not included
- Chemical-free or organic-only venues: ask us. We can fly OMRI-listed and biological-only products for an upcharge. Some venues require this in their contract; we can accommodate.
- Standing water elimination on the property (we treat with larvicide, but draining ponds and clearing gutters is on your maintenance team)
- Indoor pest control (we are an outdoor-only operation)
- Tree and shrub disease spray (different service, ask for a referral)
