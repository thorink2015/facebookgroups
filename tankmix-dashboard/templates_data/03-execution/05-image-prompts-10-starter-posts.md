## Production guidance

**Canva cards (Posts 1, 2, 5, 7):**
Build one template, save as a Canva brand kit. Bone-white background, two type styles (serif headline, sans-serif body), Tank Mix wordmark in the bottom right at consistent size. Save the template. New cards take 5 minutes after the first.

**AI hero photos (Posts 3, 4, 6, 8, 9, 10):**
Tools that work for this aesthetic:
- Sora 2 (best for natural documentary feel, but expensive credits)
- Midjourney v7 (best image quality, "stylize 200" works well for this look)
- Imagen 4 / Google Vertex (cheapest, good enough for in-feed posts)

Always generate 4 variations and pick. First-try outputs rarely land.

**Optional overlay text on hero photos:**
Keep overlay text white, sans-serif, no drop shadow, max 12 words, positioned in the upper third or lower third (never the center). For Post 8 the overlay is part of the strategy because it carries the stat into the feed.

**Tank Mix wordmark sizing:**
14-18 pixels tall on a 1080x1080. Bottom right corner, 30 pixels from each edge. Same position every time so it becomes recognized.
