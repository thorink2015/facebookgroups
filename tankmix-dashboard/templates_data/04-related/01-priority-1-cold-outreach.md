## Personalization research (5 minutes per prospect)

For each prospect, before sending:
1. **2 minutes:** Visit their website's "About" or "Blog" or "News" page. Find one specific recent thing (a product launch, a content piece, a trade show appearance).
2. **2 minutes:** Check the contact's LinkedIn for their current title and one recent post.
3. **1 minute:** Drop the one specific thing into the [ONE-SENTENCE SPECIFIC OBSERVATION] slot in the template.

Personalization rule: the observation has to be SPECIFIC and TRUE. "Saw your work" is not specific. "Your trailer setup guide gets cited in operator forums" is specific.

## Send schedule

- **Volume:** 3 to 5 sends per day, max. Anything more and your warmup work gets undone.
- **Days:** Tuesday through Thursday only for first sends. Mondays are spam-flag hot, Fridays get ignored. Follow-ups can run any business day.
- **Time:** Send between 9:30 AM and 11 AM in the prospect's time zone. Most have CRM dashboards open by then.
- **Spacing:** Don't send two sends back to back to the same email domain. Stagger 30 minutes minimum.

5 days a week at 4 a day = 20 sends a week = the 22 Priority 1 list cleared in roughly 6 business days, with bandwidth left over for the 30 Priority 2 prospects.
