# docs/ — project documents

Reference material and the place to upload your own files.

## Already here
- **PLAN.md** — the batch plan + what's needed from you next.
- **USER-REQUIREMENTS.md** — your goal and original request, saved for the record.
- **RESEARCH-FINDINGS.md** — the safety-relevant conclusions the build is based on.
- **DECISIONS-AND-LOG.md** — key decisions, the boundary, and a mistakes log.
- **compliant-distribution-research-prompt.md** — paste-ready prompt to research
  legitimate, scalable ways to grow the newsletter.

## Upload your own files here
- **`docs/research/`** — drop your raw research docs and the Tank Mix playbook here
  (gitignored by default so they stay private to your machine; remove the matching
  line in `.gitignore` if you *want* them committed).
- **`docs/uploads/`** — any other documents (screenshots, notes, exports).

## Your data goes one level up
- **Groups CSV** → keep a copy in **`../data/`** and import it from the Groups tab.
- **Images** → upload them in the dashboard's Images tab (stored in `../data/images/`).
